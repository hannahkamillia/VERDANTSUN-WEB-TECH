About Us


Save The Wildlife. You can do it too.

VerdantSun is a team that organize the wildlife conservation. In this organization, we collect the donations from the people from any party whether it is from media social, public area, school and any large manufactures. Any people can join our team and be as a volunteering that we will together commit to raise awareness of all of people and donation in relation to Sustainable Goal Development, Goal 15 which are Life on Land.

VerdantSun also functions as distributor from the donation that we received. We start an organization that focuses on the importance of sustaining and protecting wildlife. Verdant Sun is a non-profit organization that has a vision to preserve the wildlife and sustain the ecosystem. In relation to SDG, Goal 15 we decided to create a platform where we can promote our vision and mission to the world regarding the importance of preserving wildlife.
